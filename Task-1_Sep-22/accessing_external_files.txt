1.5 How can you access the external files for each lesson of this course?

->  To access external files for each lesson of a course:

    1. Check Course Resources: Look for a "Resources" or "Files" section within the course materials.

    2. Download Links: Click on the provided download links to access and download the external 
                       files associated with each lesson.

    3. Follow Instructions: Follow any specific instructions given in the course content to 
                            access or use these external files effectively.
